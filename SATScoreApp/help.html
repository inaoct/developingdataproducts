College App Help

This app allows the user to discover the following information related to college choices based on SAT scores:

  1. Given SAT scores, determine appropriate colleges to apply to, as well as link to additional information about the college
  2. Assuming admittance based on specific SAT scores, provide information on the probability of graduating

How to find and explore suitable colleges

  • Go to the College Map tab
  • Using the slider controls on the left-hand side of the screen, enter the SAT scores you would like to use
  • The map on the right-hand side will automatically update to include only universities that have median SAT acceptance scores above the scores you selected
  • If you click on a specific marker, you will be able to see the exact median SAT scores for the school, as well as the name of the school
  • If you click on the school's name, you can go directly to the web site of that school and further explore school information

How to get an estimate of graduation probability

  • Go to the Predictions tab
  • Using the slider controls on the left-hand side of the screen, enter the SAT scores you would like to use
  • The diagrams on the right-hand side (below the map) represent the results of three different models that were used to determine the probability of graduation given admittance to the school with specific SAT scores
  • The predicted probability for each model is listed below the graphs.
  • The prediction is also reflected with a circle using the color of each model
